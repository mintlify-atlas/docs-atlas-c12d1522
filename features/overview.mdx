Simple Alarm Clock comes with a comprehensive set of features designed to give you complete control over your wake-up experience. From gentle pre-alarms to customizable themes, every aspect is designed with flexibility in mind.

## Core features

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Snooze" icon="clock" href="/features/snooze">

  </Card>

## Sound and vibration

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Fade-in" icon="volume">
    Alarm volume gradually increases over 30 seconds by default
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Vibration" icon="mobile">
    Enable or disable vibration for each alarm independently
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Volume control" icon="sliders">
    Separate volume controls for main alarm and pre-alarm
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Auto-silence" icon="volume-xmark">
    Automatically silence alarms after 10 minutes by default
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Alarm management

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Repeating alarms" icon="repeat">
    Set alarms to repeat on specific days of the week
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="One-time alarms" icon="calendar-day">
    Create alarms for specific dates that delete after dismissing
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Skip notifications" icon="forward">
    Skip the next occurrence of a repeating alarm for 30 minutes
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Long-press dismiss" icon="hand-pointer">
    Prevent accidental dismissal with long-press requirement
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Display options

<Accordion title="List layouts">
  Simple Alarm Clock offers three different layouts for your alarm list:
  
  - **Bold** (default): Large, easy-to-read alarm display
  - **Compact**: More alarms visible on screen at once
  - **Classic**: Traditional alarm clock layout
  
  <Tip>
    Change your layout in Settings under "List layout" (preference key: `ui_list_row_layout`)
  </Tip>
</Accordion>

## Time formats

The app automatically adapts to your device's time format settings, supporting both 12-hour and 24-hour formats.

<Note>
  Most feature defaults can be customized in the app settings. Individual alarms can override many global settings.
</Note>
